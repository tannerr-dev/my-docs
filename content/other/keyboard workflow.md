- **Lean into long press**
- left thumb is for layer (and space maybe, right thumb needs a break)
- switch which hand does the layer key, just like shift, even out the work

- **Be intentional with each movement**
	- jump words (w, e, b)
	- big jumps (11, 22, j k)

- dont forget `super + h` or `super + l` for switching apps
	- if you limit to just 2 apps on a workspace
- utilize `a+s + j` `a+s + k` for switching workspaces

```
- term kanata in 1
- docs / webpages in 2
```

	- or `space u` for end and `space y` for home
